Game:
You start the Game as a "@" in a defined position in one of the 3 maps of the game.
You have different stats : level,health,attack,defense and experience
You can encounter monsters(health,attack,defense) on the map that can move randomly and chase you
if you are close enough.
You cono combat monsters.
If you die the game ends. 
In the first level you can battle 3 goblins and a Bandit that is blocked by a barrier,you can pick a key to open it.
Second and Third stage,it's another map with more monsters like an orc or a Dragon.
